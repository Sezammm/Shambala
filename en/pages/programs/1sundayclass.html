Sunday classes for children

The center “Shambhala” offers cultural education classes in Buryat and Kalmyk languages, national dance, national vocal, and national games for children. This project is of great importance to our community, as it will help preserve and promote the cultural heritage of the Buryat and Kalmyk people among the children, ensuring they remember and cherish their roots.

Every Saturday from 11 am to 4 pm, our center offers a wide range of engaging and educational classes tailored specifically for children. These classes provide a fun and enriching environment where kids can explore their interests, develop new skills, and make lasting memories.

Schedule:

11.00 am – 11.50 am - National dance class

12.00 pm – 12.50 pm - History of Buddhism

1.00 pm – 1.50 pm - Buryat / Kalmyk languages

2.00 pm – 2.50 pm - Drawing / national game (Shaga naadan)

3.00 pm – 3.50 pm - National vocal
