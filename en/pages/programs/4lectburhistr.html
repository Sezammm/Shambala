Lectures on Buryat history

The lecture series on Buryat history at the Shambhala Center provides an in-depth exploration of the Buryat people’s cultural and historical legacy. This course delves into significant events, influential historical figures, and the traditions that shape Buryat culture. Participants will gain a thorough understanding of the formation of the Buryat nation, its role in various historical contexts, and its influence on modern society. The series is designed to preserve and promote Buryat culture, while also enhancing knowledge of its rich historical heritage.
