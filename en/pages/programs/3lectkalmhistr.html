Lectures on Kalmyk history

The course of lectures on Kalmyk history at the Shambhala Center provides a deep and comprehensive understanding of the cultural and historical heritage of the Kalmyk people. The course covers key events, important historical figures, as well as traditions and customs of the Kalmyks. Participants will learn about the formation of the Kalmyk nation, its role in various historical contexts, and its influence on contemporary society. The course aims to preserve and promote Kalmyk culture and deepen knowledge of its rich historical past.
