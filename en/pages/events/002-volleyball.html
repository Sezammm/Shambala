Annual volleyball tournament

The Center "Shambala" is thrilled to announce its highly anticipated Annual Volleyball Tournament, set to take place on June 15-16, 2024 in New Jersey and Brooklyn. This exciting event promises to be a day of intense competition, community spirit, and fun for all ages.

The Annual Volleyball Tournament is a cornerstone of our community outreach efforts designed to foster camaraderie, promote physical activity, and offer a platform for athletes of all skill levels to showcase their talents. This year’s tournament is expected to draw teams from across the region, each eager to compete for the top honors.

Event Details:

Date: June 15-16, 2024

Time: 10.00 am

Location: 47 Readington rd, Branchburg, NJ 08876 and
Prospect park, Brooklyn, NY

Event Highlights Include:

Competitive Matches: Watch as teams battle it out on the court in a series of thrilling games.

Awards and Prizes: Celebrate the champions with trophies and prizes.

Food and Refreshments: Enjoy a variety of food and drink options throughout the day.

Community Engagement: Connect with fellow community members and enjoy a vibrant atmosphere.

“This tournament is more than just a competition; it’s a celebration of our community spirit and an opportunity for everyone to come together for a fun and memorable experience,” said the captain of the winning team. “We’re excited to see a great turnout and to share this event with our friends, families, and neighbors.”

For more information about the festival and upcoming events, please subscribe to our newsletters or follow us on our social media.
